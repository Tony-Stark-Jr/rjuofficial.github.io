RAJARSHI JANAK UNIVERSITY


Previous Next

Rajarshi Janak University was founded on October 2017 by the Legislature-Parliament pursuant to Sub-article (1) of Article 296 of the Constitution of Nepal. ...The University has been set up as a multi-disciplinary university in Province 2, having its Central Office in Janakpurdham. Rajarshi stands for the person, widely acknowledged for the highest level of wisdom. King Janak is also known as the father of Hindu Goddess Sita, in the eastern civilization of the world.

Our Moto

To avail an opportunity for accessing the qualitative higher education from all sectors in order to contribute for the national development by producing efficient professional, classical and qualitative manpower according to the need of time.

Our Goals

1. Be a center for learning through teaching, research and artistic endeavors.
2. Provide outstanding teaching, research and community services.
3. Promote technology-based, innovative and inter-disciplinary learning environment, and
4. Produce and disseminate research outcomes, teaching materials and other publications by undertaking demand-driven research on local, national and international issues and problems.

Our Mission

Rajarshi Janak University serves as the flagship for higher education being a center for both teaching and research, dedicated to excellence in higher education and enhancement of the social and economic well-being of the people of Nepal by providing technical and professional leadership through transmission of knowledge and modern state-of-art of teaching-learning methodologies. The University recognizes the importance of its location in Janakpurdham and seeks to link its research and teaching to the vast resources of a great sub-metropolis in the Province No. 2 and all over the nation. It also seeks to attract a diverse and international faculty and student body, to support research and teaching on national, regional and global issues, and to create academic relationships with many countries and regions.

Our Vision

The Vision of Rajarshi Janak University is to become among the leading quality education centers of academic excellence in teaching-learning and research in South Asia and contribute to the development of the nation and other global region. The University will be perceived and acknowledged as one of the outstanding public universities in the nation - A World Class University.

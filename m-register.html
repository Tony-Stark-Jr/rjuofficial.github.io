Message from Mr. Mahendra Prasad Yadav (Registrar)

Welcome to Rajarshi Janak University!

I have been privileged to have been appointed as the first Registrar of the government funded Rajarshi Janak University, Nepal. With the management’s supportive outlook and visionary planning; and the dynamism of the Vice Chancellor Prof. Bharat Jha, we imagine the university to steadily grow into a leading multi-disciplinary center for advanced learning. We strive to shape our students into responsible citizens through imparting quality education and coaching moral values. Our sincere objective is to provide quality education to the students and quality service to the faculties and staffs. Though established recently about two and half years, RJU has carved out a position of credibility for itself in the academic world. With passion and commitment of the university members, proactive management system, highly facilitated libraries and laboratories with advanced equipment, highly qualified faculties and staffs and the best practices in teaching and knowledge sharing processes are marked here. Our university has been emerged as a preferred destination for the students keeping choice of better academic excellence of this zone. RJU is attempting to be similar with acceptable international standards and to provide a unique learning experience to students. Adopting the enhanced global connectivity, the website of RJU enlists the need-based information on its diversified programmes. I hope you will take a few moments to visit the website. Thank You!

Registrar
